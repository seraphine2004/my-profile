Chicas in tecnologia

The combination of my interest in teamwork and my desire to make the world better led me to the «Chicas en tecnología» Argentinian project. My team and I participated in the «PUUM» program (Programando Un Mundo Mejor). The aim of the project was to raise awareness about the small number of women involved in technology. We had to identify an issue in our daily lives and create an app to fix it using MIT app inventor software.

Humanitarian

I signed up for a humanitarian project led by the Iranian photographer Reza in the “Villa31”, the biggest slum of Buenos Aires, Argentina. Children from “villa 31” were trained in photography while my schoolmates and I helped them express the message they wanted to convey with their pictures. A book of the photographs was published to highlight social inequalities, and presented to the French Embassy in Buenos Aires. It was an inspiring and eye-opening experience.
